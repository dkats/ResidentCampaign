Welcome

During the preclinical years of medical school, I realized that being a doctor is more than healing patients at the bedside. A key component is advocacy, because if I don't speak up, who will? There's a ridiculous amount of misinformation spreading through the social and news media, but we have an opportunity to counter it! This inspired me to start writing stories about patients that I feel can serve as teaching points. Writing also became a way for me to de-stress and to process some of the feelings that I have to bottle up in the moment.

Most of these are raw and emotional, so if I offend you, I'm sorry; it really wasn't the intention. Please email me and tell me why I am wrong or what I can do to improve. Part of the reason I put these thoughts on the internet is to grow as a person, so I would appreciate any and all guidance.

Thank you!
