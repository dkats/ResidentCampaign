Sex Education

I haven't done much to pursue this yet, but I have taught sex ed at middle schools in Cleveland, OH and at the Cuyahoga County Juvenile Detention Center. Currently, I'm working on creating my own curriculum (adapted from those I have taught), with the goal of creating separate lessons for children, parents, and teachers. During my teaching experiences, I've been shocked by how many kids don't know the basics of reproduction and contraception, even though many of them were already parents. The long-term dream is to create an online class (for free) to make sex ed as accessible as possible.
